About

I’m a full stack software engineer who loves facing challenges, and loves going beyond the limits that I unconsciously set for myself out of fear, weakness, and ignorance, and realizing that if I really want something, when there is a will, there is a way. Having a background in Real Estate, and also having been able to spend a year living in a Monastery, I feel confident in my ability to transfer into a software engineering role my strong communication and interpersonal skills to build meaningful and respectful relationships with others, and the spiritual and meditative side of me to keep great attention to detail and to focus and concentrate deeply to my tasks at hand.

After leaving the monastery, I developed a keen interest in software engineering. That interest took me from knowing nothing about web development, to attending an immersive and in-person coding bootcamp called App Academy for 16 weeks, and building multiple applications from scratch. The technologies I learned and implemented include JavaScript, React, Redux, Ruby On Rails, PostgreSQL, SQL, the MERN Stack, to name a few. The project that I’m most proud of is a Slack clone I built. With the use of the WebSocket communication protocol I was able to build a live chat and give to users live updates of the creation of messages, channels, and new conversations.

My desire to keep learning and growing as a developer doesn’t cease and I know that it will only increase with time. The drive, the passion, and the enthusiasm I feel for learning and improving my engineering skills give me confidence in my ability to reach my full potential as a software engineer and to build great software.
