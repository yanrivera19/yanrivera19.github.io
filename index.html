About

I love challenges. I love going beyond the limits that I unconsciously set for myself out of fear, weakness, and ignorance, and realizing that if I really want something, when there is a will, there is a way. Recently, I found myself wanting to make a career change and achieving something that I would be proud of but didn’t know which direction to take. I searched like a madman until I stumbled upon the world of coding, and instantly felt intrigued to learn and understand what it was. My first thoughts were “this is just too much”, “I should have started years ago, now it’s too late to make something out of this”. But I knew that anything worth doing would not be easy.

While doing some research I found some videos on YouTube of people that, without having a CS degree, were able to become professional software developers. I got really inspired and knew that what I wanted to do was impossible only if I believed it to be so. I finally made the decision that coding was going to be my new career path, and that I was going to do all that it took to make that happen.

And here I am.

After months of intense struggle, study and practice, I feel I have gained the necessary experience that will help me achieve my goal of getting my foot in the tech industry and becoming a professional software developer.

The Hacker News App

  • A The Hacker News clone app where you can search any news on the search bar, page through the results using the pagination at the bottom of the page and select any news you would want to see in detail by clicking it. A link containing the selected article will then be opened on a new tab.
  • Technologies used: ReactJS, Redux, News API, Axios, and Bootstrap.

    To-Do List App

  • A full-stack to-do list app where you can add, edit, or delete to-do's from a to-do list. The app has an authentication feature, so it can only be used if you're signed-in to a Google account.
  • Technologies used: ReactJS, Redux, Semantic UI, Axios, JSON Server (back-end/database), and OAuth 2.0 (authentication).
    • SEE DEMO SOURCE CODE

    Spotify App

  • A Spotify clone app where you can search for songs/tracks, artists, and albums that match whatever term you submit on the search bar. When you select a song, artist, or album, it will be opened on the Spotify website on a new tab.
  • Technologies used: React, Semantic UI, Axios, Spotify Web API.
